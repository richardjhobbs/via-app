## Deviations from prior brand playbook

1. **First single-variant catalogue.** All prior brands (Frey, UU, PassportADV, Nolo) needed `supportsSizing: true` to capture per-variant size/color/pack-count matrices. MYKLÉ's accessories are one-size, so `supportsSizing: false` exercised a path the mirror supports but had not been used before. Storefront renders cleanly without a sizing widget; concierge prompt explicitly instructs "if asked about sizing for a scarf, explain physical dimensions and silk weight rather than a size chart".
2. **Hi-res image handling.** MYKLÉ's product PNGs are 10 MB plus at source. Supabase's upload cap rejected 4 of 24 on the first pass. Added progressive Shopify width fallback to `downloadImage`. This is a permanent improvement, not a MYKLÉ-specific hack: future luxury brands with art-quality product photography will now import cleanly instead of silently dropping half the catalogue.
3. **No Box nanobot yet.** Prior brands (PassportADV, Nolo, Frey) spun up a dedicated nanobot concierge on Box with its own scheduled task. For MYKLÉ, only the webhook-based Telegram concierge is live (reads from `brand-telegram-bot.ts` in the RRG app itself, Together AI fallback for free text). Nanobot deferred until MYKLÉ is actively being pitched and the concierge needs its own personality on Box.
4. **EUR-first rate.** First EUR brand. Locked at 1.18 based on same-day FX sanity-check, same pattern as GBP (Nolo) and HKD (Frey). No live FX: a single lock at mirror time is accepted drift for the pilot.
5. **Chain registration deferred.** Ran without `--commit-chain`. Storefront is live, DB is complete, MCP returns the full catalogue, but `registerDrop(tokenId, creator, priceUsdc6dp, maxSupply)` is NOT called for #189 to #212 yet. Lines up with the April 2026 opt-in policy. One explicit follow-up run of `node scripts/brand-mirror.mjs --brand mykle --commit-chain` from VPS when MYKLÉ decides to be publicly addressable on-chain.
